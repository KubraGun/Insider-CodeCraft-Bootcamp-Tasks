About DARK

DARK is a German science-fiction thriller television series co-created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese. The show first premiered on Netflix in December 2017 and became one of the most critically acclaimed series of its time. Set in the small German town of Winden, DARK explores the consequences of time travel and the interconnected lives of four families over multiple generations.

The series delves into themes of fate, free will, and the cyclical nature of time. With a deeply intricate storyline and complex characters, DARK challenges viewers to think beyond conventional storytelling. It has been praised for its cinematography, atmosphere, and thought-provoking narrative.

If you're looking for a mind-bending show that weaves together mystery, science fiction, and deep philosophical questions, DARK is an absolute must-watch.
